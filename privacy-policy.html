Privacy Policy for Flashcards Buddy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Flashcards Buddy ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Flashcards Buddy (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users of our App. Flashcards Buddy is designed to work entirely offline and does not require user registration or personal data collection.

2.2 Usage Data

The App may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the application experience. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect device-specific information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for technical support and optimization purposes.

3. Audio and Speech Features

3.1 Text-to-Speech Functionality

Our App uses text-to-speech technology to help users learn pronunciation of words and phrases. This feature:

  • Only converts text to spoken audio (output only)
  • Does not record, store, or transmit any audio from your device
  • Uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine
  • Processes all audio locally on your device

3.2 Audio Permissions

The App may request audio-related permissions as required by the Android operating system for text-to-speech functionality. However, we want to be clear that:

  • We do not record any audio from your microphone
  • We do not access or store any audio files from your device
  • All speech synthesis happens locally on your device
  • No audio data is transmitted to our servers or third parties

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Mobile Ads

Our App uses Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data in accordance with their privacy policy. For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Expo Platform

Our App is built using the Expo platform. Expo may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve their services. For more information, please visit: https://expo.dev/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

All user progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This data includes:

  • Learning progress and statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • Favorite flashcards
  • Achievement data

5.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our App does not collect personal information from any users, it is safe for children to use under parental supervision.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear app data through your device settings to reset all local progress
  • Disable text-to-speech features in the app settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. International Users

Our App is available globally. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device and we do not collect personal information, there are no international data transfer concerns.

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
